Find Photos in Albums

Click the "Find in Album" button on the main screen, the search interface is as shown below. First, please select the beginning and end dates of the photos, then optionally fill some keywords in the title, author, location, paragraph text search box, then click the search button. The following example is to search for photos taken in Bitan over a period of 10 years. If you want to abort the search, you can click the "Cancel" button next to the progress bar on the lower right corner.

The example result is shown below. Album EPUB Browser lists the matched photo paragraphs, and indicate the chapters of the albums where they are from. Click on a photo, the view box appears for you to view and download the original image file. Click on the "More..." links next to the titles of the album or chapter, and you go to that album or chapter to read it.

Lastly, it should be emphasized that the cross-album search function does not recognize the contents of the photos "in the images". Instead, the user has already labeled the date, location, and the story of the photos by text properly when he creates the albums with Album EPUB Studio. This search function detects the text information, so that the results can be obtained quickly.
