Read an Album

Open an Album File

To read the album content, just (1) click an album file item in the list, (2) click the "Read Album" button or double click the album item in the list directly.

To open an EPUB album file out of the working directory, you don't need to copy or move it to the directory. As shown below, just click the "Browse Files" button, and the file dialog box appears for you to select an album file. Or directly click the file in the Windows Explorer, right-click on it, and select "Open" or "Open with", then choose Album EPUB Browser to open it.

Read the Chapters

When the album is opened, the screen is shown as below. You can click the chapter you want to read on the left side, then the content of the chapter is presented as a web page on the right side. Click any photo, the view box pops out, and you can download the original image file of the photo by clicking "Save As".
