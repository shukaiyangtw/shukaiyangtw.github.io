Create the Album Cover

Some templates of album cover are provided in the software. To apply the templates, click the toolbar button Generate the Cover in the album info window, then you see the cover-generation window, and the selected photo is shown in the middle of cover. Next, you (1) select a background image, (2) select the Text Font and Size (only TrueType fonts supported), (3) pick the Text Color, and (4) Generate the cover.

The generated cover is shown as above. If you don't want the selected photo, click the toolbar button Remove the Photo. When you are back to the album info window, you can see the generated cover in the left area. In you want to use your own image instead of the generated cover, click the toolbar button My Cover Image to upload an image file. If you don't want an album cover, click the toolbar button Remove the Cover.
