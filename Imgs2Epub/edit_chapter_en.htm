Edit the Chapter

As mentioned, the chapters in an EPUB book are basically web pages. A page usually contains headings (h1 and h2 headings) and many paragraphs. In the user interface of the software, a paragraph consists of a brief description and a set of photos. The photos in a paragraph are usually taken at the same date and location. On the generated page, every paragraph shows the text description followed by the thumbnails of photos. Between the paragraphs, there can be optional sub-headings.

Enter the Chapter Editing

In the album info window, as shown below, (1) pick the chapter to be edited in the table of contents, then (2) click the toolbar button Edit the Chapter, or just double click on the chapter. You enter the chapter-editing window.

When a new chapter is created by the software, a new blank paragraph is also created for the user to add content immediately.

Edit the Text and Add Photos

The chapter-editing window is shown below. In the left is the list of paragraphs, and in the right is the content of the selected paragraph. The first photo in a paragraph is shown in the left list, and the editable content of a paragraph is shown in the right including the date and location, the text description, and the photos.

To add content in a paragraph, first (1) pick the paragraph in the left list, (2) you can give a subheading to the paragraph optionally. (3) Check the box if you want to display the date and pick the data by the calendar. (4) Check the box if you want to display the location and fill the text field. (5) Write a brief text description of the photos. (6) Click the toolbar button Add Photo Files to browse photo files, of just drag and drop photo files in the photo set area. You can pick or drag multiple photo files, then the software generates the thumbnails automatically. Anytime, you can preview the result page by toolbar button Preview the Album. If you want to delete a photo from the paragraph, pick it and click the toolbar button Delete the Photo.



As the shown preview, the software decides the size of thumbnails according the amount of photos. For typesetting, the software makes 2:1 thumbnails for landscape photos, and makes 1:2 thumbnails for portrait photos. Clicking on a thumbnail shows the original photo in both the preview and the generated EPUB book.

Add or Delete Paragraphs

To add a new paragraph in the chapter, as shown below, just (1) click the toolbar button New Paragraph, then a new blank paragraph is appended to the list. (2) Click the new paragraph, then you can fill the content and add photos in the right area. If you want to delete some paragraph, click the paragraph in the list and click toolbar button Delete this Paragraph.

In the previous example, the heading of paragraph is left blank. In this case, you (3) filled the heading, then you can Previewthe Album as shown below.

Reorder Photos and Paragraphs

In the chapter-editing window, the user can reorder the photos and paragraphs by mouse dragging. You can (1) move a paragraph by dragging it in the left list. You can (2) move a photo dragging it in the photo set. And you can (3) move a photo to another paragraph by dragging it into the paragraph in the left list. After dragging, you can Previewthe Album immediately.

There are more functions for individual photo in the next topic.
