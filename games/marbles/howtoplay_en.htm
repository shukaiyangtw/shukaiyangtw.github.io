HOW TO PLAY

Introduction

It is a game to eliminate all marbles within the time limit, as shown in the figure, the player sets the direction of the arrow by mouse, mousetouch screen, or a keyboard, then shoot an emoji marble. If a cluster of three or more marbles of the same emoji is hit, they are eliminated and scored. In addition to the current marble on the arrow, the next marble will also be placed at the lower right corner, so the player can switch them to get a better score.

Besides, there are two types of special marbles. One is a marble marked +5, which gains an additional 5 seconds when eliminated The other is a bomb marble. When it hits somewhere on the screen, the neighboring marbles will be eliminated together regardless of color.

Eliminate all marbles within the time limit to pass the level and advance to the next level. The number of marbles eliminated by one shot and the remaining seconds are bonus points. When the time runs out or the accumulated marbles reach the edge, the game will be over.

Instructions

Mouse

Simply left-click anywhere on the screen and the current marble will shoot out in that direction, and right-click to swap the current and the next marbles. In addition, you can also intentionally click on the empty areas on either side to make the current marble bounce off the left and right boundaries, in order to hit the marbles that are blocked in the back rows and get a higher score.

Touch Screen

The operation on the touch screen of the mobile device is the same as that of the mouse. Tap any position of the screen, the current marble launches in that direction. If you want to exchange the marbles, just tap the next marble at the lower right corner.

Keyboard

When playing with the keyboard, use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the arrows, press the down arrow key to exchange marbles, and then press the blank key to shoot out the current marble. The mentioned keyboard and mouse functions can be used at the same time in the game.
